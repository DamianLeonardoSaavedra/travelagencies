About Us

Built on a love for travel — and a head for paperwork

Patagonia Dreamers began with a simple idea: travel shouldn't be complicated by logistics. Based in Tripoli, Libya, we help travellers book flights, students apply for study visas, and visitors make their way to Libya.

Founded by Juancito Doe, Patagonia Dreamers grew out of years spent helping friends and family navigate embassy queues, airline booking systems, and confusing application forms. What started as informal favours grew into a full-service travel agency.

Today, we work with people leaving Libya for international trips and study programmes, as well as visitors from abroad who need a visit visa to experience Libya. Wherever you're going, our goal stays the same: clear guidance, honest timelines, and a trip that goes smoothly from the first booking to the final stamp.

No call centres, no endless hold music — just a small team that picks up the phone and answers your emails.
